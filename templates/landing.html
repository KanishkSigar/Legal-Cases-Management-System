Everything a firm tracks, in one place

Three roles, each with a tailored view — enforced at the database query level.

Case management

Case numbers, types and a full status workflow (Open → Won/Lost), with a notes timeline on every case.

Appointments

Schedule meetings between lawyers and clients, optionally tied to a case, with one-click complete / cancel.

Lawyers & clients

Full profiles — specialisation, bar number, contact details — each linked to a secure login account.

Role-based access

Admins see all; lawyers see only their cases; clients see only their own — scoped in the ORM, not just the UI.

Built around a relational core

The whole system is a handful of well-normalised tables. Foreign keys keep every record consistent.
